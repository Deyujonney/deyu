About Me

  • Deyu Zhang (06/03/1987) is an associate professor with School of Computer Science and Engineering in Central South University (CSU). He received his Ph. D in Central South university. He recevied the B.Sc. degree from PLA Information Engineering University in 2005; and M. Sc degree from Central South University in 2012, China, all in Communication Engineering.
  • His research interests include system optimization in edge computing, and resource allocation in green sensor/IoT networks.
